What is Rain Water Harvesting?

Rainwater harvesting is the process of collecting and storing rainwater for later use. It involves capturing rainwater from rooftops, open grounds, or other surfaces and directing it into storage tanks or underground reservoirs. This collected water can then be used for various purposes, such as:


  • 1. Watering plants and gardens: Rainwater is an excellent source of water for plants as it is free of chemicals and minerals that can harm them.
  • 2. Flushing toilets: Using rainwater for flushing toilets can significantly reduce the amount of potable water used in homes.
  • 3. Washing clothes: Rainwater can be used for washing clothes, especially in areas with hard water.
  • 4. Cleaning purposes: Rainwater can be used for cleaning floors, cars, and other surfaces.
  • 5. Recharging groundwater: Rainwater can be infiltrated into the ground to recharge groundwater aquifers, helping to maintain water levels during dry periods.
